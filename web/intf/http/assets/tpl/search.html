General Operators

Operators allow searching for specific product properties. The allowed operations on each Operator depend on the property type. value can be text, number(e.g. 3.141), date/time (e.g. 2020-01-01 10:30:45), or time duration (format: 1y2M3w4d5h6m7s, e.g. 1d12h).

operator:value
operator:`word1 word2` 		Equal Text
operator:==value Equal
operator:!=value Unequal
operator:<value Less
operator:<=value Less or Equal
operator:>=value Greater
operator:>=value Greater or Equal
operator:value..value Range (inclusive, e.g. 2..*, 2022-01-01..2022-04-01 )
